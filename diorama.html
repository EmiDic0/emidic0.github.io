The Fisherman's House

The fisherman is a simple man that enjoys simple things such as hikes, catching fish, and flying his beloved seaplane.

This diorama of a scenic fisherman's house was made over the span of two months. The modeling was made in Autodesk Maya, and the textures were made from pictures in photoshop.
