Who Am I?

My name is Emiliano Dichter, and i am a second year student at Howest Digital Arts and Entertainment, in the Independent Game Production major. I was born in Italy, but I've been living in Belgium for quite a while. My passion for game development comes from my curiosity for how the world around us is made. I was so interested in how my favourite media was produced, that I made the decision to pursue it as a career.

On this website, you will find a collection of the projects I'm the most proud of, along with a section in which you will learn to know me better.
