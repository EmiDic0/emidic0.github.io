Kill the witch

Dodge attacks, gather potions and survive long enough to kill the witch.

This small arcade game was made by me in 2 months and a half. The game was made in Monogame, using the C# language, and all of the sprites and assets were made by me in aseprite. The goal of the game is to dodge all the witch's attacks and gather purple potions to damage her. If you wish to play the game, you can download it here just extract the folder, and launch KTW.exe.
