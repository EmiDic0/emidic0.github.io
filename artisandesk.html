The Artisan's Desk

After a long night of work, a tired astrologer goes to sleep, but doesn't bother to clean up his desk.

This project was made in a month and a half, and was made entirely by me. I first modeled every item individually in blender, before assembling them in the scene. I then textured every item in Adobe Substance 3D Painter, and finally assembled the scene in the Unity Game Engine.
