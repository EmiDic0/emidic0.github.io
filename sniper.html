The Anti-Material Rifle

Want something dead? This is the weapon you want. The Anti-Material Rifle is a weapon designed to neutralize even heavily armored targets from an incredible distance.

Every aspect of this weapon was made by me, from the model to the textures and the animation. I followed a similar workflow than with the robuddy and the rocket kart, going from the high polygon model to the low polygon version, and finally baking the high polygon details onto the low polygon model. The Anti-Material Rifle was made in Blender, and the textures were made in Adobe Substance 3D Painter using PBR (Physically Based Rendering) materials.
