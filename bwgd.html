Bunny Wars

In this game, you play as the hero of the garden, a bunny! Save bunny town from running out of water by defeating the four minions of the evil beaver that are blocking the water with their dams. Acquire new abilities and cards as you defeat enemies in this turn-based deckbuilder game.

This strategy deckbuilder is the result of a two months school assignement, where we had to design a game from scratch in teams of 5, thinking about the mechanics, game design, art and programming. My role in the theme was the lead programmer. I coded almost everything in the game. I also actively helped the designing and refining of the mechanics. The game was made in Unity, and all the assets were made by us. If you want to try it out for yourself, you can find it on itch.io.
